About Us

Serving Humanity with Compassion & Dignity

New Life Charitable Foundation was established with a deep desire to serve humanity and create positive social impact. Our organization was founded by Mr. Basavarajappa, who envisioned a platform where people from different walks of life could come together to help the needy and contribute toward community development.

Over the years, the foundation has expanded its activities and outreach to serve children, women, senior citizens, and homeless individuals in various areas including Kannur, Kothanur, Kammanahalli, Lingarajapuram, Geddalahalli, and surrounding localities. We work closely with local communities to understand their challenges and provide practical support that addresses real-life needs.

Our Vision

Building a Compassionate & Empowered Society

Our vision is to build a compassionate and empowered society where every child receives education, every woman has the opportunity to become self-reliant, and every needy individual receives care, support, and dignity.

We envision communities where poverty does not stop children from learning, where women are financially independent, and where vulnerable individuals are treated with respect and kindness. Our goal is to create sustainable change through education, empowerment, awareness, and humanitarian service.

Our Mission

What We Strive to Achieve

  • Provide quality educational support to children from economically weaker backgrounds
  • Empower women and school dropout girls through skill development and income-generating opportunities
  • Support homeless and needy individuals through food distribution and welfare programs
  • Spread awareness about health, hygiene, education, and social responsibility
  • Promote environmental and social sustainability through reuse and redistribution initiatives
  • Encourage compassion, volunteerism, and community participation

Our Commitment

Transparency, Accountability & Service with Compassion

We are committed to transparency, accountability, and service with compassion. Every contribution made to our organization directly supports programs that improve lives and bring hope to struggling families. At New Life Charitable Foundation, we believe that true development happens when communities are empowered from within. Our initiatives encourage participation, dignity, and self-confidence among the beneficiaries.
