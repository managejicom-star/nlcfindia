Welcome to New Life Charitable Foundation

Bringing Hope, Dignity, and Opportunity to Every Life

New Life Charitable Foundation is a non-governmental organization dedicated to serving underprivileged communities and transforming lives through education, empowerment, compassion, and social responsibility. Founded with a vision to create a society where every individual has access to basic needs, learning opportunities, and a dignified life, our organization works tirelessly among vulnerable families, children, women, and homeless individuals.

Based in Chikkagubbi, Karnataka, we believe that even small acts of kindness can create meaningful and lasting change in society. Through our educational support programs, women empowerment initiatives, awareness campaigns, food drives, and community welfare projects, we are continuously striving to build a brighter and more hopeful future for those in need.

Our Founder

Meet Mr. Basavarajappa

New Life Charitable Foundation was established by Mr. Basavarajappa, who envisioned a platform where people from different walks of life could come together to help the needy and contribute toward community development. His vision and compassion have touched countless lives across Kannur, Kothanur, Kammanahalli, Lingarajapuram, Geddalahalli, and surrounding areas.
